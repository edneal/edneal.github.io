How we do it

Phase 1: Thinking

We begin by meeting with your team to determine your communication objectives. We apply our creative process to your needs and draft a short document called a treatment. The treatment briefly describes the proposed program concept and flow. The treatment includes a description of the proposed video production approach, as well as the estimated cost. Once the treatment is approved, the project moves to phase two.

Phase 2: Pictures

We schedule the video recording dates and direct the sessions. We provide an edit schedule and together with you determine the review steps. Your feedback from each review is essential to the process. Typically, we present a review draft at three points: rough cut, final cut, and release cut.

We work to engage your viewer through three program dimensions:

  • Utility. The information has take-away value
  • Character. The program expresses integrity and pride
  • Sensation. The program taps the emotions

Our Approach

We approach your needs through two broad production packages. Our basic package offers a simple, fast, low-cost style of production. Our extended package is aimed at high-end production needs.

Basic

High definition recording to AVCHD file format. Crew consists of cameraperson/director. The basic package is great for:

  • Tutorials
  • Documentaries
  • Video blogs
  • Product profiles
  • Proposal support messages
  • Award recognitions
  • Video for PowerPoint
  • Interviews & Testimonials
  • Video biographies

portal-to-portal, $750.

Extended

XDCam High-definition production.
